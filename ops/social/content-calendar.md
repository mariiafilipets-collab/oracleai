# Content Calendar Template

## Weekly cadence

- Mon: Product update + key metric snapshot
- Tue: Feature deep-dive thread
- Wed: Community highlight
- Thu: Market/event teaser
- Fri: Weekly recap + weekend CTA
- Sat/Sun: Light engagement formats

## Channel mapping

- X: short updates, threads
- Telegram: release notes and alerts
- Discord: community prompts and discussions
- Instagram: visual cards and reels captions
- TikTok: short concept scripts and hooks
